Student Life

Extracurricular Activities


Our extracurricular activities at O.B. Montessori Center support the development of our students’ personalities so they grow up confident, productive, and disciplined.



Camping


In our camping programs, students immerse in nature to build resilience, work in teams, and develop leadership skills through outdoor survival training activities.




Visual Arts


Our arts programs encourage creativity and self-expression in students by allowing them to explore various mediums and showcase their work in school exhibits.




Sports


Physical education and sports are integral parts of our curriculum. We offer a variety of sports programs that promote physical fitness, wellness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.




Music


Our music programs allow students to discover and develop their musical abilities, from learning instruments to participating in the choir and band.




Theater and Dance


The theater and dance programs of O.B. Montessori Center expose the students to the world of the performing arts as they work with experts and top talents in professional Philippine theater and dance. These programs help build awareness and appreciation of Philippine culture and heritage so students experience how the arts affect and benefit society.
