Course Offers

The Course Offers we give at O.B. Montessori Center are what helps the students to make their future better and brighter



CASA

(Pre-Kindergarten to Kindergarten)


The Casa Program encourages the transformation of children from ages 3 to 5 as they consciously and willingly learn from their environment.




Primary

(Grade 1 to 3)


In the Primary Grade School Program, from ages 6 to 8 until they achieve independence from adults and gain confidence as they relate to the world.




Intermediate

(Grade 4 to 6)


In the Intermediate Grade School Program, the students from ages 9 to 12 find their responsibility while they enhance academic proficiency while facilitating teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership skills.




Junior High School

(Grade 7 to 10)


Our Junior High School Program from ages 13 to 16, focuses on strengthening critical skills like problem-solving, collaboration, and communication by harnessing their creative energies as they begin to seek economic independence.




Senior High School

(Grade 11 to 12)


The Senior High School Program further equips the students ages 17 and 18, the curriculum is GAS Plus, which allows the students flexibility in exploring career options and seamlessly moving up to the tertiary level of any local or international college or university.
